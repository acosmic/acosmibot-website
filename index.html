Games & Gambling

Keep your community entertained with interactive games and a complete economy system.

  • Coinflip, Deathroll, Rock Paper Scissors
  • Magic 8Ball predictions
  • Weekly lottery system
  • Player vs Player betting games
  • Slot machine gambling

Leveling & XP System

Reward active members with experience points, levels, and automatic role assignments.

  • Participation-based leveling
  • XP points for chatting & commands
  • Auto-applied Discord roles
  • Interactive leaderboards
  • Member statistics tracking

Economy System

A complete fake currency system with credits, banking, and member-to-member transactions.

  • Credit-based fake currency

  • Weekly lottery for credit recovery
  • Economy leaderboards
