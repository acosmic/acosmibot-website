Mines

Reveal safe tiles, grow your multiplier, and cash out before you hit a mine.

How Mines Works

Start a game with /mines <bet> <mines>. Acosmibot creates a hidden 25-tile board with your chosen number of mines. Each safe reveal increases the cash-out value. Revealing a mine ends the game and loses the bet.

Command

/mines <bet> <mines>

Examples:

/mines 100 3 /mines 1000 10 /mines 25000 24

Rules

  • Bets must be between 100 and 25,000 credits.
  • Mine count must be between 1 and 24.
  • You can cash out after revealing at least one safe tile.
  • If the game times out after a safe reveal, Acosmibot automatically cashes out.
  • If the game times out before any reveal, the bet is forfeited.

Stats

Mines results are included in /stats-games, including total games, wins, losses, wagered credits, net profit, and biggest win.
