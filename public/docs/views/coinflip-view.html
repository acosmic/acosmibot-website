Coinflip

A simple 50/50 bet — pick a side, place your wager, and let the coin decide your fate.

Overview

Coinflip is the purest gambling game in Acosmibot's economy — no strategy, no house edge tricks, just a straight 50/50 shot. Pick heads or tails, name your price, and double up if the coin lands in your favor. It's fast, simple, and endlessly satisfying (or agonizing).

Key Features

  • True 50/50 odds — no hidden house advantage
  • Win double your bet on a correct guess
  • 10% of losing bets flow into the guild vault
  • Minimum bet of 1 credit keeps it accessible
  • Instant results — no waiting around

How to Play

1

Choose Your Side

Decide between Heads or Tails. This is your prediction for which side the coin will land on.

2

Place Your Bet

Enter the amount of credits you want to wager. The minimum bet is 1 credit and you cannot bet more than your current wallet balance.

/coinflip Heads 500
3

See the Result

The coin is flipped instantly. If your guess is correct, you win double your bet. If you're wrong, your bet is lost — 10% of it goes to the guild vault.

Commands

/coinflip <side> <bet>
Flip a coin — choose Heads or Tails and place your bet. Win double on a correct guess. Minimum bet is 1 credit.

Examples:

/coinflip Heads 100 /coinflip Tails 2500

Payouts & Loss Mechanics

Outcome What Happens
Win You receive double your bet (net gain = bet amount)
Loss Your bet is deducted; 10% of it is added to the guild vault

🏦 Guild Vault

The guild vault accumulates credits from losses across all gambling games. Server admins can distribute these funds as event prizes, giveaways, or rewards — creating a natural credit sink that keeps the economy healthy.

Tips

💡 Playing Smart

  • Coinflip is purely luck — no strategy changes the odds
  • Use smaller bets to stretch your credits further across many flips
  • Bank a portion of your winnings after a big flip to protect your gains
  • Don't chase losses — the odds reset to 50/50 every single flip
  • Great for settling friendly disputes between members too!

Troubleshooting

Q: The command says I don't have enough credits

A: Your bet cannot exceed your wallet balance. Use /balance to check your current credits. If you have credits in the bank, use /withdraw first.

Q: I bet below the minimum and got an error

A: The minimum bet is 1 credit. Make sure your bet amount is at least 1.

Q: Is coinflip truly 50/50?

A: Yes. Each flip is an independent random result with equal probability for heads and tails. Previous outcomes have no effect on the next flip.

Q: Where does the 10% from my loss go?

A: It goes to the server's guild vault. Server admins control how those funds are used — typically for events or community giveaways.
