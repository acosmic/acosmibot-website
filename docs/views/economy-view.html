Economy & Banking System

A fully-featured currency system with credits, daily rewards, transfers, and a global bank for your community.

Overview

Acosmibot's economy system provides a virtual currency called "credits" that members can earn, spend, and transfer. The system integrates with games, rewards, and other features to create an engaging server economy.

Key Features

  • Daily credit rewards with streak bonuses
  • Credit transfers between members
  • Global bank for deposits and withdrawals
  • Integration with gambling games
  • Transaction history tracking
  • Leaderboards for richest members

How It Works

üí∞ Credits

Credits are the virtual currency in Acosmibot. Members start with 0 credits and can earn them through:

  • Daily Rewards: Claim free credits every 24 hours
  • Gambling Games: Win credits by playing slots, lottery, blackjack, etc.
  • Transfers: Receive credits from other members
  • Admin Grants: Server admins can give credits as rewards

üè¶ Global Bank

The bank allows members to store credits safely. Banked credits cannot be lost in gambling games until withdrawn. This encourages members to save their earnings.

  • Deposits: Store credits in the bank
  • Withdrawals: Take credits out for spending
  • No Interest: Bank balance doesn't grow automatically
  • Safe Storage: Banked credits are protected

Commands

/balance [member]
Check your current credit balance and bank balance. Optionally view another member's balance.

Examples:

/balance /balance @username
/daily
Claim your daily credit reward. Build up streaks by claiming daily without missing a day for bonus rewards.

Example:

/daily
/transfer <member> <amount>
Send credits to another member. The amount is deducted from your balance and added to theirs.

Example:

/transfer @username 500
/deposit <amount>
Deposit credits from your wallet into the bank for safe keeping.

Examples:

/deposit 1000 /deposit all
/withdraw <amount>
Withdraw credits from the bank to your wallet for spending.

Examples:

/withdraw 500 /withdraw all
/richest [scope]
View the richest members leaderboard. Choose between server-only or global rankings.

Examples:

/richest /richest server /richest global
/givecredits <member> <amount>
Grant credits to a member. Requires administrator permissions. Use for events, contests, or rewards.

Example:

/givecredits @winner 10000
/removecredits <member> <amount>
Remove credits from a member. Requires administrator permissions. Use for penalties or corrections.

Example:

/removecredits @username 500

Daily Rewards & Streaks

How Daily Rewards Work

Members can claim free credits once every 24 hours using /daily. The base reward is 100 credits, but this can be increased with:

  • Consecutive Days Streak: Claim daily without missing a day to build streaks
  • Streak Bonuses: Higher streaks = bigger rewards
  • Streak Reset: Missing a day resets your streak to 1

Example Streak Progression

Streak Days Bonus Multiplier Total Reward
1 day 1.0x 100 credits
7 days 1.5x 150 credits
14 days 2.0x 200 credits
30 days 3.0x 300 credits

Transaction History

All credit transactions are tracked in the database, including:

  • Daily reward claims
  • Transfers between members
  • Bank deposits and withdrawals
  • Gambling wins and losses
  • Admin grants and removals

üìä Viewing Your History

Transaction history can be viewed through your user dashboard at /dashboard. This shows recent transactions and your balance over time.

Troubleshooting

Q: I can't claim my daily reward

A: You can only claim /daily once every 24 hours. Check when you last claimed it.

Q: My streak reset but I didn't miss a day

A: Streaks require claiming within 24 hours of your last claim. If you claim at 11pm one day and 1am the next, you have a very short window the following day.

Q: Can I transfer credits between servers?

A: No, credits are server-specific. However, your global balance (total across all servers) is tracked for leaderboards.

Q: What happens if I go into negative credits?

A: You cannot go negative. Gambling games will prevent bets if you don't have enough credits.
