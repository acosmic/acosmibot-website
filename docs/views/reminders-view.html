Reminders

Never forget anything — set a reminder and Acosmibot will DM you the moment the time arrives.

Overview

The reminder system lets any server member schedule a personal message to be delivered at a future time. Reminders are sent as a direct message from the bot so they reach you wherever you are, even if you've left the server in the meantime.

Key Features

  • Set reminders up to 5 years in the future
  • Flexible time format — combine units like 2h30m or 1d12h
  • View and manage all your active reminders at any time
  • Cancel individual reminders by ID before they fire
  • Delivered as a DM so you never miss it

Commands

/remind <time> <message>
Set a reminder for a future time. The bot will DM you with your message when the time arrives. Time can use any supported format or a combination of units.

Examples:

/remind 30m Check the oven /remind 2h30m Team meeting starts soon /remind 1d Take out the trash /remind 1w Server event this time next week
/reminders
View a list of all your active reminders, including each reminder's ID, message, and the time remaining until it fires.

Example:

/reminders
/cancelreminder <id>
Cancel an active reminder before it fires. Use /reminders to find the ID of the reminder you want to remove.

Example:

/cancelreminder 42

Time Format Reference

Time values are flexible and human-friendly. You can use shorthand abbreviations or write them out, and combine multiple units in a single value.

Unit Accepted Formats Example Input
Seconds s 30s
Minutes m, min 5m, 15min
Hours h, hr 2h, 3hr
Days d 1d, 7d
Weeks w, week 2w, 4week
Years y, yr 1y, 2yr
Combined Multiple units chained 2h30m, 1d12h

Combining Time Units

  • 2h30m — 2 hours and 30 minutes from now
  • 1d12h — 1 day and 12 hours from now
  • 1w2d — 1 week and 2 days from now
  • 1y6m — 1 year and 6 months from now

How It Works

1

Set your reminder

Run /remind <time> <message> in any channel where the bot is active. The bot confirms your reminder with a timestamp showing exactly when it will fire.

2

Go about your day

The bot tracks the timer in the background. You don't need to stay in the server or keep Discord open — the reminder will be waiting when you return.

3

Receive your DM

When the time arrives, Acosmibot sends you a direct message with your original reminder text so you know exactly what you asked to be reminded about.

Limits & Notes

Things to Keep in Mind

  • Maximum reminder time is 5 years in the future
  • Reminders are personal — only you receive your own DMs
  • Make sure your DMs are open to receive the reminder message
  • If the bot is offline when a reminder fires, it will be sent as soon as the bot comes back online

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I didn't receive my reminder DM

A: Check that your Discord privacy settings allow DMs from bot accounts. In User Settings → Privacy & Safety, ensure "Allow direct messages from server members" is enabled, or add Acosmibot as a friend to allow DMs directly.

Q: Can I set multiple reminders at once?

A: Yes. You can have as many active reminders as you like. Use /reminders to see them all listed together.

Q: Can other people see my reminders?

A: No. Your reminders are private — only you can view them with /reminders, and only you receive the DM when they fire.

Q: What if I leave the server before my reminder fires?

A: The reminder will still be delivered to you via DM as long as the bot can message you.
