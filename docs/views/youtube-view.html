YouTube Integration

Get notified when your favorite creators upload videos or go live on YouTube with customizable announcements.

Overview

The YouTube integration uses PubSubHubbub (WebSub) to receive instant notifications when channels upload videos or start live streams. Configure custom announcements with role mentions to keep your community updated.

Key Features

  • Real-time notifications for uploads and live streams
  • PubSubHubbub subscription for instant updates
  • Customizable announcement messages per channel
  • Role mentions (@everyone, @here, or specific roles)
  • Video metadata in announcements (title, thumbnail)
  • Automatic webhook management

How It Works

1

Add YouTube Channels

Configure which YouTube channels to track through the /server dashboard. Enter the channel name or ID.

2

PubSubHubbub Subscription

Acosmibot subscribes to YouTube's feed for each channel. When new content is published, YouTube sends a webhook notification.

3

Content Verification

The bot checks if the content is a new upload or live stream (not a scheduled premiere or re-upload).

4

Announcement Posted

An announcement is posted to your designated channel with video details, thumbnail, and any role mentions.

Troubleshooting

Q: Notifications aren't working

A: Verify that YouTube integration is enabled, announcement channel is set, and the channel name/ID is correct.

Q: I'm getting notifications for old videos

A: This can happen when first adding a channel. Future uploads will work correctly.
