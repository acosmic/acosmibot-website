Leveling System

Engage your community with an XP-based progression system featuring customizable formulas, automatic role rewards, and level-up announcements.

Overview

The leveling system rewards server activity by granting XP (experience points) to members when they send messages. As members accumulate XP, they level up and can be automatically assigned roles based on their level.

Key Features

  • Customizable XP formulas (Linear, Quadratic, Exponential)
  • Automatic role assignment at specified levels
  • Level-up announcements with custom messages
  • Daily leaderboard announcements
  • Streak tracking for consecutive daily activity
  • XP multipliers and level caps

How It Works

1

Members Earn XP

Members earn XP by sending messages in your server. The amount earned is based on your configured XP multiplier and anti-spam protection prevents XP farming.

2

XP Accumulates to Levels

As members accumulate XP, they progress through levels. The XP required per level is determined by your chosen formula:

3

Automatic Role Rewards

When a member reaches a configured level milestone, they automatically receive the assigned roles. You can choose between different role assignment modes.

4

Announcements

Level-up achievements can be announced in a designated channel with customizable messages that support placeholders for member mentions, levels, and more.

Role Assignment Modes

Progressive Mode
Members keep all previous role rewards as they level up. Each new level adds more roles without removing old ones.

Example:

Level 5: Bronze ‚Ä¢ Level 10: Bronze + Silver ‚Ä¢ Level 20: Bronze + Silver + Gold
Additive Mode
Similar to progressive, but you have more control over which roles stack and which replace previous ones.
Selective Mode
Only the highest level role is assigned. Previous level roles are automatically removed when reaching a new tier.

Example:

Level 5: Bronze ‚Ä¢ Level 10: Silver (Bronze removed) ‚Ä¢ Level 20: Gold (Silver removed)

Troubleshooting

Q: Members aren't gaining XP

A: Ensure the leveling system is enabled in your server dashboard. Also check that the bot has permission to read messages in the channels.

Q: Level-up announcements aren't working

A: Verify that you've selected an announcement channel and that the bot has "Send Messages" and "Embed Links" permissions in that channel.

Q: Roles aren't being assigned automatically

A: Make sure the bot's role is higher than the roles it's trying to assign. Check the role hierarchy in Server Settings ‚Üí Roles.

Q: How do I reset all levels?

A: Contact support for bulk level resets. Individual resets can be done with /setlevel.
